Los Angeles County, California

Cases in Los Angeles County started rising rapidly during the months of November 2020 to January 2021. On December 18, 2020, the first vaccination went into effect with the FDA issuing an Emergency Use Athorization (EUA) for the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine. This seemed to mitigate the spread until a new strain was discovered on November 2, 2021 thus explains the rise in cases from months December 2021 to January 2022. On November 29, 2021 the CDC recommends the public to get the recent released COVID-19 booster explaining the stagnant growth of cases during the month of January 2021. As for the deaths, there is no rapid growth since the vaccination was first released so it appears the vaccination has saved many lives.
