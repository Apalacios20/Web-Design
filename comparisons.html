County Comparisons

In comparisons, all three of the Counties follow a similar trend in both the cases and deaths category. As we all know, New York was the first major city hit in the U.S. as its cases skyrocketed as early as the month March, 2020. There was not a gradual build as shown for the other two counties whose cases seemed to take off in the month December 2020. Both cases and deaths seem to be stagnant at the end of the timeline but if the graphs tell us anything, it is that once another strain comes along, and they seem to, cases and deaths will rise again till yet another booster is released.
